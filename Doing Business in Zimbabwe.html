Obstacles to Businesses in Zimbabwe¶

Understanding constraints to firms is important as the nation prepares to vote for a new government.

Access to Finance is the most frequently listed as the main obstacle to doing business in Zimbabwe. 22.5% of the firms surveyed list it as a constraint. Close second and third are Practices of Competitors in the Informal Sector (21.5%) and Political Instability (17.5%), respectively. In fact, these top 3 obstacles are listed by 61.5% of all firms surveyed.

This is consistent with my (limited) knowledge of Zimbabwe. The ongoing cash crisis originally brought about by the debasing of the currency from hyperinflation has led to difficulties in accessing hard currency to facilitate imports. Hopefully the political change from 2017 will help pave the way for continued political stability and certainty for would-be investors. The political contenders would do well do heed what the data tells us.
